Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our ghostwriting services.

How does the ghostwriting process work?

Our process begins with an in-depth consultation to understand your goals. We then match you with a writer who conducts interviews and research to capture your voice. You'll receive drafts for feedback throughout the process until we deliver the final product.

What's the typical cost for ghostwriting services?

Costs vary based on project scope, length, and complexity. Book projects typically range from $25,000 to $100,000. We offer various pricing models including flat project fees, hourly rates, and retainer agreements.

Will my ghostwriter sign an NDA?

Absolutely. All our writers sign comprehensive NDAs before beginning any project. Your ideas and information remain completely confidential.

How long does a typical book project take?

Most book projects take 6-12 months from initial consultation to final manuscript. The timeline depends on the book's length, research required, and your availability for interviews and feedback.
